Modals – Complete Explanation

1. Can

Meaning: Ability, permission, possibility

  • Jab kisi kaam ko physically ya mentally kar sakte ho
  • Jab kisi se casually permission lena ho
  • Possible situation batani ho

Examples:
He can solve this problem.
Can I borrow your pen?

Trick: Jo kaam confidently abhi possible ho, ya permission chahiye, “can” lagao.

2. Could

Meaning: Past ability, polite request, possibility

  • Kaam jo past mein kar paate the
  • Zyada polite tarike se baat karna
  • Weak possibility dikhani ho

Examples:
When I was younger, I could run fast.
Could you shut the door, please?

Trick: “Can” ka past ya polite version “could” hai. Softness dikhata hai.

3. May

Meaning: Permission, possibility

  • Formal/official permission lena ya dena
  • Jab kuch hone ki sambhāvnā ho

Examples:
May I come in?
It may rain today.

Trick: Formal request ya decent prediction mein “may” lagao.

4. Might

Meaning: Less certain possibility

  • Bahut kam chance ho kisi cheez ke hone ka

Example:
He might come to the party.

Trick: “May” se bhi kam surety ho toh “might” lagao.

5. Must

Meaning: Necessity, compulsion, strong advice

  • Zaruri ya majboori dikhani ho
  • Strong suggestion ya logical conclusion

Examples:
You must wear a seatbelt.
He’s not at home; he must be at the market.

Trick: Majboori, rules, ya pakka conclusion – “must” lagao.

6. Shall

Meaning: Future intention, offers, suggestions

  • Formal offers, suggestions
  • Apne future ki baat karna

Examples:
Shall I carry your bag?
We shall meet tomorrow.

Trick: Offers, suggestions, ya apne future ke promise mein “shall” aata hai.

7. Should

Meaning: Advice, moral duty, mild obligation

  • Kisi ko salah dena
  • Mann ki baat (what is right)

Examples:
You should eat healthy food.
People should respect elders.

Trick: Jab salah ya guidance deni ho, “should” lagao.

8. Will

Meaning: Definite future, willingness, promise

  • Future plan ya promise
  • Readiness dikhana

Examples:
I will call you later.
She will help you.

Trick: Sure future ya pakka promise in English means “will”.

9. Would

Meaning: Past habit, polite request, imaginary condition

  • Polite baat
  • Past routine
  • “If” ke saath imagination

Examples:
Would you like some tea?
When I was a child, I would visit my grandma every summer.

Trick: “Will” ka polite ya past form, ya imagination mein “would” lagta hai.

10. Ought to

Meaning: Moral/strong advice

  • Jab duty ya strong “should” ki feeling ho

Example:
We ought to help the poor.

Trick: Jo kaam morally correct ho, “ought to” use karo.

11. Need

Meaning: Necessity

  • Zaruri cheez bataane ke liye

Examples:
You need not worry.
Need you leave now?

Trick: Mostly negative ya question mein use hota hai (“need to” positive mein)

12. Dare

Meaning: Challenge ya himmat dikhana

  • Negative ya question mein

Examples:
Dare you do this?
He dare not speak the truth.

Trick: “Dare” rarely use hota hai, bold ya challenge wala tone ho toh.
